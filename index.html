Captain America

Steve Rogers



Like many upstanding superheroes, Captain America is often seen as the pinnacle of what humans should strive to be, being the best possible version of ourselves with a strict moral code and a general willingness to always do what is right. The character traits that define Steve Rogers not only elevate him into hero-worship by fans and other characters alike but also give us something to strive for. He’s a character that is overall good, but he does have his own weaknesses and flaws that he has to overcome. This makes him a much more interesting character than he would have been if he was just a simplistic Boy Scout stereotype.

Introverted

Steve Rogers as an individual pretty much keeps his own counsel. If there are things to be done, he’ll be leading the way.
